    BANKING & FINANCIAL SERVICES

    Banking operations have become so vast today in terms of their scope and data usage that they have no option but to automate their processes. The more the level of automation in a banking process, the more risky it becomes. These transactions are exposed to tremendous levels of possible fraud and foul play. The information interchange that happens in finance institutions is of the most sensitive nature and hence requires the strongest security measures in place. With the face of banking changing from a brick mortar model to an e-commerce or even an m-commerce model, the need for integrated security systems has never been higher.

    Banks face great pressure to roll out newer additions to their services in narrow time frames to maintain an edge over competition. However the last thing a bank wants to do is to compromise on its security features.

    ❝ We have had a rich and fruitful experience of dealing with many banks and various forms of financial institutions in building absolutely impenetrable security systems which are customized around optimising their processes and daily operations. These security systems consist of information risk management processes for their individual risk profiles and their operational structures.


    While building our solutions we abide by the leading international standards and regulations such as BS 7799/ISO 27001, ITIL, Basel Committee guidelines etc.
